Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform.

Suppliers set their own prices, which are typically much lower than market rates (often just 10-30% of original cost). Some items may even be offered for free. The platform facilitates direct communication between institutions to agree on terms.

All hazardous materials must be properly labeled with SDS (Safety Data Sheets) included. We partner with certified chemical logistics providers who specialize in safe transport of hazardous materials. Both suppliers and requesters must confirm they have appropriate storage facilities before transacting with hazardous chemicals.

We verify each college through official email domains, cross-checking with UGC/AICTE databases, and sometimes requiring documentation from department heads. This ensures all participants are legitimate academic institutions, maintaining trust and safety within our network.

Basic membership is free for all verified colleges. We charge a small service fee (5% of transaction value, capped at ₹1000) only on completed transactions to cover platform maintenance and development costs. There are never any listing fees or subscription charges.

While we facilitate connections between institutions, all arrangements and agreements are made directly between the participating colleges. We recommend clear communication and documentation between parties.
